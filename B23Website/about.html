The B23 Story

Our Story

We started B23 ("Bee" "Two" "Three") to help customers see through the hype and uncertainty of Big Data. B23 provides a new source for Big Ideas, and most importantly provides capabilities to implement those big ideas.

We are currently based in the Washington, D.C. area, but we will travel anywhere a customer is in need!

    Our Customers

    The B23 team has been supporting large, small, commercial, international, and government customers in a variety of roles for almost two decades. We have designed, implemented, operated, and managed the most complex network, datacenter, and application infrastructures in the world.

    We never take our customers for granted as they are our greatest source of inspiration.

    Our People

    B23 is a people-company, and we recognize that our people are our greatest asset. We promote a culture of customer-centricity, technical acumen, and autonomy to make the right decisions to promote Big Ideas.

    We aim to reward our employees for making the right decisions to support our customers, and for being the best in the world.
